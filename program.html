Our Programs

Join us in shaping a better tomorrow with initiatives designed to empower children in need.

AAKAR - The First Step

"Shaping the future by laying a strong foundation for every child."

Focus on early childhood development through activities, play-based learning, and personality development. Activities include basic literacy programs, storytelling sessions, and interactive workshops.

Aim: To build confidence, curiosity, and creativity in children at an early age.

AHAR APURTI

"Nourishing hope with every meal."

Dedicated to providing nutritious meals and eliminating hunger among children in orphanages. Includes food donation drives, meal planning for health, and collaboration with local food banks.

Aim: To ensure no child sleeps hungry and promote a balanced diet for physical growth.

AVSAR - A Chance

"Unlocking potential through opportunities."

Focused on creating opportunities for children through education, skill development, and extracurricular activities. Includes vocational training, career counseling, and talent showcases.

Aim:To help children discover their talents and prepare for a self-sufficient future.

LAKSHYA

"Guiding dreams to reality."

A program to mentor and guide children toward achieving their ambitions. Offers goal-setting workshops, access to scholarships, and career-focused mentoring programs.

Aim: To nurture aspirations and empower children to dream big.

UPHAAR - Gift a Smile

"Bringing joy through small acts of kindness."

Focused on encouraging donations of toys, books, clothes, and other essentials. Also includes organizing special events like birthday celebrations and festive gatherings for children.

Aim: To foster happiness and spread smiles among children.
